

TITLE:	City of Gold

OVERVIEW:	An anguished media magnate, Jonathan Davenport, accompanies his estranged lover to the Peruvian Amazon in pursuit of a reclusive artist living in rebel occupation. Despite their philanthropic intentions, the mission proves to be the harbinger of something dark and ominous rooted deep within Jonathan.

RELEASE DATE:	04-12-2018		

GENRE: Adventure
