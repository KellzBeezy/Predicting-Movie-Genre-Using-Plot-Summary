

TITLE:	Children of the Corn II: The Final Sacrifice

OVERVIEW:	Arriving in Gatlin, Nebraska, a news-reporter and his son get wind of a story about the youth in the town murdering their parents finds that a series of brutal murders are revealed to be worshipers of the corn-stalks and try to stop them before they carry out their plans.

RELEASE DATE:	29-01-1993		

GENRE: Horror
