

TITLE:	Shrooms

OVERVIEW:	A group of American teens comes Ireland to visit an Irish school friend who takes them on a camping trip in search of the local, fabled magic mushrooms. When the hallucinations start taking hold, the panicked friends are attacked by ghostly creatures; never able to determine if they are experiencing gruesome reality or startling delirium.

RELEASE DATE:	16-08-2007		

GENRE: Horror
