

TITLE:	Rest Stop: Don't Look Back

OVERVIEW:	One year after running away from home, Nicole and Jesse are still missing. When Jesse's brother, Tom, returns home from active duty, he sets out with his friends Marilyn and Jared to locate the lost couple. Their search leads them to the stretch of old highway with a mysterious Rest Stop, where they find themselves in the same predicament as Nicole and Jesse: confronting a madman.

RELEASE DATE:	30-09-2008		

GENRE: Horror
