

TITLE:	The Curse of the Werewolf

OVERVIEW:	Conceived by the rape of a mute servant girl by a demented beggar, an infant is raised by a wealthy but kindly bachelor-gentleman and his woman servant who learn, years later, that he is a werewolf, a demon-possessed being whose only chance for normalcy will be, as a young man, to find a woman who will love him for himself, which will effect his release from this curse.

RELEASE DATE:	01-05-1961		

GENRE: Horror
