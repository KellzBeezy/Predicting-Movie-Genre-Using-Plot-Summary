

TITLE:	The Return of the Vampire

OVERVIEW:	In 1918, an English family is terrorized by a vampire, until they learn how to deal with it. They think their troubles are over, but German bombs in WWII free the monster. He reclaims the soul of his wolfman ex-servant, and assuming the identity of a scientist who has just escaped from a concentration camp, he starts out on a plan to get revenge upon the family.

RELEASE DATE:	11-11-1943		

GENRE: Horror
