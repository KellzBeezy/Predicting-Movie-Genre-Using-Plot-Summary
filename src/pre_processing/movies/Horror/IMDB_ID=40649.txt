

TITLE:	Demonic Toys

OVERVIEW:	A botched bust on a pair of arms dealers inadvertantly leads to the raising of a sixty-six-year-old demon with the power to bring toys to life as his personal minions. The demon is looking for a body to inhabit so he can increase his powers, and it just so happens that one of the police officers is pregnant with the ideal host.

RELEASE DATE:	12-03-1992		

GENRE: Horror
