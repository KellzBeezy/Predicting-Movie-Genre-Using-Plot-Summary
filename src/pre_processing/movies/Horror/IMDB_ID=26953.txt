

TITLE:	Puppet Master

OVERVIEW:	Toulon, a puppet maker, discovers an ancient Egyptian potion and uses it to bring his creations to life. His puppets become murderous little demons and escape. Distraught, the master takes his life. Years later, psychics tune into the existence of the monstrous marionettes and set off to stop them.

RELEASE DATE:	12-10-1989		

GENRE: Horror
