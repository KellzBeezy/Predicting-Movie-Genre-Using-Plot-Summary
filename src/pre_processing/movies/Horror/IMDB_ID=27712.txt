

TITLE:	Killjoy 2: Deliverance from Evil

OVERVIEW:	In this sequel, two juvenile detention officers must escort a group of criminally-inclined urban kids to the country where they are being forced to renovate a shelter for teens as punishment for their crimes. After one of them is shot by a local, the survivors seek refuge in the home of a voodoo woman, where they mistakenly summon legendary clown-faced demon Killjoy who begins hunting them down one by one.

RELEASE DATE:	08-01-2002		

GENRE: Horror
