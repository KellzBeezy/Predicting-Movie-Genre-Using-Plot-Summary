

TITLE:	A Virgin Among the Living Dead

OVERVIEW:	A girl arrives from London to visit her estranged relatives in a remote castle for the reading of her father's will. After a while she discovers that they are all in fact dead and her decision to live with them turns into a nightmare. Unable to leave she's drawn into a macabre underworld through visions of nude satanic rituals and her own impending sacrifice.

RELEASE DATE:	15-11-1973		

GENRE: Horror
