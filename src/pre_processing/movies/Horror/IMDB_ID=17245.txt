

TITLE:	Mulberry Street

OVERVIEW:	A mysterious virus, carried by mutant rats, creates pandemonium in New York City in which rat-bite victims turn into homicidal rat-mutants. This movie centers on six tenants of a small apartment complex on the Lower East Side Mulberry Street as they fight to survive the night against the rats and rapidly multiplying cannibal mutants.

RELEASE DATE:	23-05-2006		

GENRE: Horror
