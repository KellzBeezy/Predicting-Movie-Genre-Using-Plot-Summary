

TITLE:	Zombie Flesh Eaters

OVERVIEW:	On the Caribbean island of Matul, white doctor David Menard is trying to stem the tide of cannibal zombies that are returning from the dead.  Arriving on the island are Anne and reporter Peter West who are looking for Anne's missing father.  The pair soon find themselves under attack from the zombies.  They eventually escape in a sailboat, only to discover that the zombie plague has spread across the globe.

RELEASE DATE:	25-08-1979		

GENRE: Horror
