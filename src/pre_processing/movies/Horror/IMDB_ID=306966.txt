

TITLE:	Summer Camp

OVERVIEW:	A group of people have just signed up to be camp Councillors at a foreign country.  They expect the camp to be the place for a memorable summer.  Instead, something strange is going on and some of the campers begin to act strange.  Things go terribly wrong real fast as a terrible game of tag has the campers running for their lives or going after the campers.

RELEASE DATE:	13-05-2016		

GENRE: Horror
