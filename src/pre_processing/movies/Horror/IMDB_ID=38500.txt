

TITLE:	Howling IV: The Original Nightmare

OVERVIEW:	An author who was sent to the town Drakho, because of a nervous breakdown, gets wound up in a mystery revolving around demons and werewolves.  She starts seeing ghosts and dismisses them as her own imagination, but when they turn out to be real she becomes suspicious of the odd town and of its past.

RELEASE DATE:	01-11-1988		

GENRE: Horror
