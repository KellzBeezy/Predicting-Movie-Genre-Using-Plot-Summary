

TITLE:	Kaw

OVERVIEW:	On the last working day of Sheriff Wayne, his small town is attacked by blood thirsty ravens that eat human flesh. Meanwhile his wife Cynthia visits a farm where a Mennonite family lives to say farewell to her friend Gretchen and discloses a dark secret about the origin of the fierce ravens.

RELEASE DATE:	07-04-2007		

GENRE: Horror
