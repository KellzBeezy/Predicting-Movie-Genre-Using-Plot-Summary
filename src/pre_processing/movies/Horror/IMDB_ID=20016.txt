

TITLE:	Trailer Park of Terror

OVERVIEW:	Six troubled high school students and their chaperon, an optimistic youth ministries Pastor, return from an outdoor character building retreat in the mountains. During a raging storm, their bus crashes, hopelessly stranding them in the middle of the Trucker's Triangle, a forgotten locus of consummate evil in the middle of nowhere.

RELEASE DATE:	21-10-2008		

GENRE: Horror
