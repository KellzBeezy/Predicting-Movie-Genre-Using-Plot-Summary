

TITLE:	The Mummy's Curse

OVERVIEW:	After being buried in quicksand for the past 25 years, Kharis is set free to roam the rural bayous of Louisiana, as is the soul of his beloved Princess Ananka, still housed in the body of Amina Mansouri, who seeks help and protection at a swamp draining project.

RELEASE DATE:	22-12-1944		

GENRE: Horror
