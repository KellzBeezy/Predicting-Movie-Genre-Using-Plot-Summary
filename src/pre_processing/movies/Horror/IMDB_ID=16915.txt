

TITLE:	Gutterballs

OVERVIEW:	A brutally sadistic rape leads to a series of bizarre gory murders during a midnight disco bowl-a-rama at a popular bowling alley. One by one, players of two teams meet blood-drenched gruesome deaths at the hand of a black bowling-gloved masked killer. This alley runs red with blood by sunrise.

RELEASE DATE:	01-01-2008		

GENRE: Horror
