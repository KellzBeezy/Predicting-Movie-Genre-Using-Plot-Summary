

TITLE:	Burial Ground

OVERVIEW:	Professor Ayres discovers a secret in an ancient stone and when he opens a crypt, he revives zombies that kill him. He had invited three couples to visit him in his manor to reveal his discovery. However, they never get around to meeting the professor. Out of the blue, the zombies attack them and they seek shelter in the mansion.

RELEASE DATE:	23-01-1981		

GENRE: Horror
