

TITLE:	Shadowless Sword

OVERVIEW:	A Wuxia adventure out of South Korea, The Legend of the Shadowless Sword is a handsome martial arts epic by Kim Yung-jun (Flying Warriors). The film's simple story allows for exceptionally creative action sequences about every three to four minutes, while simultaneously building a noble tale full of faith, love, and sacrifice.

RELEASE DATE:	18-11-2005		

GENRE: Action
