

TITLE:	Azumi 2: Death or Love

OVERVIEW:	Young assassins Azumi and Nagara continue their mission to prevent a civil war. In their hunt for Masayuki Sanada, who is protected by both an army and a dangerous clan, they meet Ginkaku, a person who shows a remarking resemblance with former friend Nachi.

RELEASE DATE:	12-03-2005		

GENRE: Action
