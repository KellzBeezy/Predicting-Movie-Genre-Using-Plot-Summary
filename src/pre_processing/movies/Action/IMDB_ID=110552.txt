

TITLE:	The Protector 2

OVERVIEW:	Kham is the last in long line of guards who once watched over the King of Thailand's war elephants. Traditionally, only the perfect elephants could successfully help defend the throne, after his harrowing quest to retrieve the elephants, Kham returns to his village to live in peace. But for someone as good in martial arts as him, peace is but a wishful thought...

RELEASE DATE:	23-10-2013		

GENRE: Action
