

TITLE:	Invincible Dragon

OVERVIEW:	The undercover agent with dragon tattoo Kowloon (Max Zhang) continually helped the police to solve mysterious cases, which made him known as a rising star. However, his impulsive personality dragged him into endless troubles. He got himself into fights with the Macau detective, as well as an American Army veteran Alexander who has a secret linkage to Kowloon’s background…

RELEASE DATE:	16-05-2019		

GENRE: Action
