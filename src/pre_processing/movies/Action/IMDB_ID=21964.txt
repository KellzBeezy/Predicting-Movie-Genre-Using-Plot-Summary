

TITLE:	Five Fingers of Death

OVERVIEW:	A young boxer joins a martial arts school to increase his skill so he can enter a martial arts competition. He leaves the school when he hears that a local gangster is terrorizing the town. He comes to the aid of a young singer and brings on the wrath of the local gang. He eventually enters the martial arts competition after learning iron palm technique and takes out all competition.

RELEASE DATE:	26-05-1972		

GENRE: Action
