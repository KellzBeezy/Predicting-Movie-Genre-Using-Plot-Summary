

TITLE:	The Big Boss

OVERVIEW:	Chen is a city boy who moves with his cousins to work at an ice factory. He does this with a family promise never to get involved in any fight. However, when members of his family begin disappearing after meeting the management of the factory, the resulting mystery and pressures force him to break that vow and take on the villainy of the Big Boss.

RELEASE DATE:	02-10-1971		

GENRE: Action
