

TITLE:	Grey Gardens

OVERVIEW:	Edie Bouvier Beale and her mother, Edith, two aging, eccentric relatives of Jackie Kennedy Onassis, are the sole inhabitants of a Long Island estate. The women reveal themselves to be misfits with outsized, engaging personalities. Much of the conversation is centered on their pasts, as mother and daughter now rarely leave home.

RELEASE DATE:	27-09-1975		

GENRE: Documentary
