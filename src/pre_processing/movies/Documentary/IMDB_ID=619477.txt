

TITLE:	Театр времен Геты и Камы

OVERVIEW:	The film tells about a unique creative and family duet, about two outstanding theater directors - Henriette Janowska and Kama Ginkas. For thirty years, they continue to build an amazing theater house - not only for themselves and their actors, but also for hundreds of viewers loyal to them. This house is called the Moscow Youth Theater. The film is a family portrait in the interior of the theater and in the "exterior" of time.

RELEASE DATE:	06-08-2019		

GENRE: Documentary
