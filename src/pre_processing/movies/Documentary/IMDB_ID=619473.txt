

TITLE:	Спитак. 30 лет одиночества

OVERVIEW:	Spitak. Thirty years ago a terrible earthquake happened here. Tens of thousands died, and the survivors seemed forever frozen in time. The father and son of Manukyan live in front of the huge Spitak cemetery - and it seems that some unknown force does not allow them to escape from hopelessness and poverty.

RELEASE DATE:	05-08-2019		

GENRE: Documentary
