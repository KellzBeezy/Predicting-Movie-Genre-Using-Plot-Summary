

TITLE:	Life Itself

OVERVIEW:	Life Itself recounts the surprising and entertaining life of renowned film critic and social commentator Roger Ebert. The film details his early days as a freewheeling bachelor and Pulitzer Prize winner, his famously contentious partnership with Gene Siskel, his life-altering marriage, and his brave and transcendent battle with cancer.

RELEASE DATE:	04-07-2014		

GENRE: Documentary
