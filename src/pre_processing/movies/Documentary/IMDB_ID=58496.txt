

TITLE:	Senna

OVERVIEW:	Senna's remarkable story, charting his physical and spiritual achievments on the track and off, his quest for perfection, and the mythical status he has since attained, is the subject of Senna, a documentary feature that spans the racing legend's years as an F1 driver, from his opening season in 1984 to his untimely death a decade later.

RELEASE DATE:	07-10-2010		

GENRE: Documentary
