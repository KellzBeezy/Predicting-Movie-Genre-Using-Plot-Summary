

TITLE:	Paris Can Wait

OVERVIEW:	A woman at a crossroads traveling to Cannes along with her successful film producer husband, finds herself on a two-day road trip with his business associate. What follows is a carefree journey replete with diversions involving picturesque sites, fine food and wine, humor, wisdom and romance - reawakening Anne's senses and a new lust for life.

RELEASE DATE:	12-09-2016		

GENRE: Romance
