

TITLE:	Daytime Shooting Star

OVERVIEW:	Fifteen-year-old country girl Suzume Yosano has to move to Tokyo to live with her uncle due to her father's transfer. She bumps into a mysterious man who ends up taking her to her uncle's place after she gets lost. Turns out, Suzume will be seeing him a lot more often once she starts school because... he's her homeroom teacher?!

RELEASE DATE:	24-03-2017		

GENRE: Romance
