

TITLE:	Geetha Govindam

OVERVIEW:	Vijay Govind, a young college lecturer who dreams of marriage falls for Geetha, a level-headed woman who's wary of strangers and isn't easy to convince. While things seem to go smoothly, Vijay makes a terrible mistake which not only derails any hope for his love story, but also potential repercussions with his family.

RELEASE DATE:	15-08-2018		

GENRE: Romance
