

TITLE:	Friend Zone

OVERVIEW:	In this world, there are many people who seem to be wandering along a relationship border-lining 'friends' and 'lovers'. This borderline is also commonly known as the FRIEND ZONE. It is a special area for those who are stuck in the middle where they cannot really stay friends with their close friends, nor move forward to be their friends' lovers.

RELEASE DATE:	14-02-2019		

GENRE: Romance
