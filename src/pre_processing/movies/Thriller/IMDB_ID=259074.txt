

TITLE:	Joy Ride 3

OVERVIEW:	Rusty Nail is back on the road again looking to punish injustice at every turn - and this time it's with a group of hotheaded street racers on their way to the Road Rally 1000. As they drive through a desolate shortcut on the way to the race, an encounter with Rusty turns sour and soon he is tracking, teasing and torturing them until the end of the road.

RELEASE DATE:	03-06-2014		

GENRE: Thriller
