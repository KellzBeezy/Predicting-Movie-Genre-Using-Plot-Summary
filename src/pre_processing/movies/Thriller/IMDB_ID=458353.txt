

TITLE:	High Heel Homicide

OVERVIEW:	As a string of brutal murders plague the city, two undercover detectives (Brent Harvey and Mia Li) race to solve the mystery and unmask the killer. Their only clues: each victim is a former cop and each has a blood-red letter painted on their body. How many will have to die before the killer spells out their message in blood?

RELEASE DATE:	20-06-2017		

GENRE: Thriller
