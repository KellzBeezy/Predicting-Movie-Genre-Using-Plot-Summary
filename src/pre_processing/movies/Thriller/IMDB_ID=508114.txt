

TITLE:	Pimped

OVERVIEW:	Over the course of one night, Sarah meets the handsome, captivating and smooth talking Lewis, who aims to be the bait in a sick sexual trap set up by himself and his low-life, rich kid housemate Kenneth. When their ruse backfires, Lewis and Sarah are forced into an unlikely alliance with deadly consequences.

RELEASE DATE:	24-08-2018		

GENRE: Thriller
