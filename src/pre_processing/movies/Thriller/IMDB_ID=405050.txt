

TITLE:	Bad Girl

OVERVIEW:	Bad girl Amy, 17, is given one last chance by her adoptive parents, who think Amy's friendship with local girl Chloe is a step in the right direction. But when Amy discovers Chloe's secret she finds herself fighting for her life, and for the future of the family she herself tried to destroy.

RELEASE DATE:	11-08-2016		

GENRE: Thriller
