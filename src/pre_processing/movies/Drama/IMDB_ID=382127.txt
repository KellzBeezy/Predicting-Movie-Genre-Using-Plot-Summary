

TITLE:	Manifesto

OVERVIEW:	An outstanding tribute to various (art) manifestos of the nineteenth and twentieth century, ranging from Communism to Dogme, in connection with thirteen different characters, including a homeless man, a factory worker and a corporate CEO, who are all played by Cate Blanchett. A striking humorous audio-visual experience.

RELEASE DATE:	28-10-2015		

GENRE: Drama
