

TITLE:	The Barefoot Contessa

OVERVIEW:	Has-been director Harry Dawes gets a new lease on his career when independently wealthy Kirk Edwards hires him to write and direct a film. They go to Madrid to find Maria Vargas, a dancer who will star in the film. Millionaire Alberto Bravano takes Maria from Kirk. Count Vincenzo Torlato-Favrini takes Maria from Alberto.

RELEASE DATE:	29-09-1954		

GENRE: Drama
