

TITLE:	Rambling Rose

OVERVIEW:	Rose, is taken in by the Hillyer family to serve as a 1930s housemaid so that she can avoid falling into a life of prostitution. Rose's appearence and personality is such that all men fall for her, and Rose knows it. She can't help herself from getting into trouble with men.

RELEASE DATE:	10-09-1991		

GENRE: Drama
