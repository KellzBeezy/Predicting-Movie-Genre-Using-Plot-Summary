

TITLE:	Manon of the Spring

OVERVIEW:	In this, the sequel to Jean de Florette, Manon (Beart) has grown into a beautiful young shepherdess living in the idyllic Provencal countryside. She plots vengeance on the men whose greedy conspiracy to acquire her father's land caused his death years earlier.

RELEASE DATE:	19-11-1986		

GENRE: Drama
