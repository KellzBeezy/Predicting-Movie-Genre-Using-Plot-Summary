

TITLE:	Vincent Wants to Sea

OVERVIEW:	Vincent, a young man who suffers from Tourette's syndrome, has just lost his mother. His father, a successful politician, does not want to take care of him and therefore places him into a mental institution. He is put into a room with Alexander, a guy with a compulsive disorder, and is shown around by Marie, an anorectic girl.

RELEASE DATE:	21-04-2010		

GENRE: Drama
