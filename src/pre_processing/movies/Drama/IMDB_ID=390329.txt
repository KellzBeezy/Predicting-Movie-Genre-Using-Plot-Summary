

TITLE:	Madly

OVERVIEW:	Madly explores love in all its permutations in six short films from a vibrant group of filmmakers representing Japan, Argentina, the UK, the US, India, and Australia. All forms of love are on display in this anthology. And all manners of feelings expressed from jubilance to depression are done so strongly.

RELEASE DATE:	14-04-2016		

GENRE: Drama
