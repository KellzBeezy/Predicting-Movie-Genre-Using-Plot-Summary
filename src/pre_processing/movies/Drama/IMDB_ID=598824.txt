

TITLE:	The Guard of Auschwitz

OVERVIEW:	Nazi occupied Poland, during the World War II. Hans, a former brilliant student, has become an SS officer stationed at the Auschwitz-Birkenau concentration camp. When he is commissioned by his superior officer to build an efficient gas chamber, Hans, facing the harsh reality, begins to realize the magnitude of the atrocious acts of which he is being accomplice.

RELEASE DATE:	28-05-2018		

GENRE: Drama
