

TITLE:	Alaska

OVERVIEW:	Nadine is a French young woman. Fausto is an Italian young man trying to make it in Paris as a restaurant waiter. They accidentally meet at a five-star hotel. Both are fragile, alone and obsessed with the idea of an unattainable happiness. Their intense love is challenged by their own individual ambition and desperation.

RELEASE DATE:	05-11-2015		

GENRE: Drama
