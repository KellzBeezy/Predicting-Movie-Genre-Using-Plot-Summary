

TITLE:	Anita

OVERVIEW:	Anita is a girl of only 16 years, and while she has developed early physically, to a mature woman, she is psychologically completly ruthless. Her relationship the her parents and her friends is very poor and she is regarded with contempt by the people around her, even by the men who so ruthlessly use her.

RELEASE DATE:	25-12-1973		

GENRE: Drama
