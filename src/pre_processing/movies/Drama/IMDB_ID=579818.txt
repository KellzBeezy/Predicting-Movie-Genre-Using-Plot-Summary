

TITLE:	Я свободен

OVERVIEW:	Seventeen-year-old Artem arrives at a camp on the seashore between shifts to hide from problems and hard memories. But instead of the desired solitude of the hero, the strange inhabitants of the camp are met, and he is forced to communicate with them. A string of random (or not at all random?) Events should either break or heal the hero.

RELEASE DATE:	07-08-2019		

GENRE: Drama
