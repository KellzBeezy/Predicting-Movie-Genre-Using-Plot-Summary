

TITLE:	Flashbacks of a Fool

OVERVIEW:	An aging Hollywood star, Joe Scott, lives a life of narcissistic hedonism, observed by his laconic personal assistant, Ophelia. The death of his childhood best friend, Boots, takes our protagonist, and the movie, into an extended flashback to a sea-side town in 1970's Britain.

RELEASE DATE:	17-10-2008		

GENRE: Drama
