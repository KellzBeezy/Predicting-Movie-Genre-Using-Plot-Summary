

TITLE:	The Finishers

OVERVIEW:	Julien, 17, is wheelchair-bound due to cerebral palsy. Despite their love for him, his family is gradually falling apart under the strain of dealing with his disability. In a bid to bond with his father, Julien challenges him to participate with him in the Ironman race in Nice (French Riviera), a triathlon in which his father has previously competed.

RELEASE DATE:	26-03-2014		

GENRE: Drama
