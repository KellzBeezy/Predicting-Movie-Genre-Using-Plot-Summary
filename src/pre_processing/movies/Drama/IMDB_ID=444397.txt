

TITLE:	The Workshop

OVERVIEW:	Antoine has agreed to follow a writing workshop where some young people in insertion must write a black novel with the help of Olivia, a recognized novelist. The work of writing will bring to life the working-class past of the city. Its shipyard is closed for 25 years, a nostalgia that does not interest Antoine, influenced by the anxiety of the present world

RELEASE DATE:	11-10-2017		

GENRE: Drama
