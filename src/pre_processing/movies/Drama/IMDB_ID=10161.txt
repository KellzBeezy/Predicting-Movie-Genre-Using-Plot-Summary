

TITLE:	My Left Foot: The Story of Christy Brown

OVERVIEW:	In this true story told through flashbacks, Christy Brown is born with crippling cerebral palsy into a poor, working-class Irish family. Able only to control movement in his left foot and to speak in guttural sounds, he is mistakenly believed to have a intellectual disability for the first ten years of his life.

RELEASE DATE:	21-07-1989		

GENRE: Drama
