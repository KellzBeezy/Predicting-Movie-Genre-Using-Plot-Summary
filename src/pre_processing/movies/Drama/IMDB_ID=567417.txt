

TITLE:	Es gilt das gesprochene Wort

OVERVIEW:	While Kurdish gigolo Baran dreams of a future in Europe, German pilot Marion is struggling to come to terms with her cancer diagnosis. When the two meet at the Turkish holiday resort of Marmaris, they engage in a kind of double-cross and decide to enter into a sham marriage. After a promising beginning, a shared future seems well within their reach. But things turn out not to be quite as simple as that.

RELEASE DATE:	01-08-2019		

GENRE: Drama
