

TITLE:	Paths

OVERVIEW:	Andreas and Martin share all the ups and downs of everyday life, and their son is maturing. A cautious approach to the traces of a long relationship. The love story of two people. Not about how they come together, not a phase they go through, but all of their shared experiences: all the years between the first kiss and today.

RELEASE DATE:	01-02-2017		

GENRE: Drama
