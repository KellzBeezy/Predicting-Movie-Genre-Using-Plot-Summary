

TITLE:	The Boys Are Back

OVERVIEW:	When the wife of sports-writer Joe Warr dies of cancer, he takes on the responsibility of raising their 6-year-old son, and his teenage son from a previous marriage. As Joe rejects the counsel of his mother-in-law and other parents, he develops his own philosophies on parenting.

RELEASE DATE:	25-09-2009		

GENRE: Drama
