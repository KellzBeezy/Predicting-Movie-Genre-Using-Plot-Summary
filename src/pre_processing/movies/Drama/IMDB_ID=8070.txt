

TITLE:	The Green Room

OVERVIEW:	Based on the Henry James short story "The Altar of the Dead", in which a man becomes obsessed with the many dead people in his life and builds a memorial to honor them. This film is also based on other short story by Henry James, "The Beast of the Jungle". It would be the last film Truffaut would act in.

RELEASE DATE:	05-04-1978		

GENRE: Drama
