

TITLE:	The Etruscan Smile

OVERVIEW:	The Etruscan Smile stars acclaimed British actor Brian Cox as Rory MacNeil, a rugged old Scotsman who reluctantly leaves his beloved isolated Hebridean island and travels to San Francisco to seek medical treatment. Moving in with his estranged son, Rory sees his life transformed through a newly found bond with his baby grandson.

RELEASE DATE:	12-04-2018		

GENRE: Drama
