

TITLE:	A Cry in the Dark

OVERVIEW:	Based on the true story of Lindy Chamberlain: During a camping trip to Ayers Rock in outback Australia, she claims she witnessed a dingo taking her baby daughter, Azaria, from the family tent. Azaria's body is never found. After investigations and two public inquests, she is charged with murder. The case attracts a lot of attention, turning it into a media sideshow.

RELEASE DATE:	11-11-1988		

GENRE: Drama
