

TITLE:	Steam: The Turkish Bath

OVERVIEW:	Francesco and Marta run a husband-and-wife design company in Rome. When Francesco's aunt dies in Instanbul he travels there to sort out the hamam turkish steam bath that she left him. He finds a love and warmth in his realtives' Instanbul home that is missing from his life in Italy.

RELEASE DATE:	24-10-1997		

GENRE: Drama
