

TITLE:	Phoebe in Wonderland

OVERVIEW:	The fantastical tale of a little girl who won't - or can't - follow the rules. Confounded by her clashes with the rule-obsessed world around her, Phoebe seeks enlightenment from her unconventional drama teacher, even as her brilliant but anguished mother looks to Phoebe herself for inspiration.

RELEASE DATE:	07-02-2008		

GENRE: Drama
