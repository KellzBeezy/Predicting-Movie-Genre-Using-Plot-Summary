

TITLE:	Hostages

OVERVIEW:	Soviet Georgia, 1983. Preparations for Nika and Ana's wedding are in full swing and it's a big day for both of their elite families. For the newlyweds and their friends, however, the celebrations are in fact part of a cover-up, as they plot an audacious escape from the Soviet Union.

RELEASE DATE:	20-04-2017		

GENRE: Drama
