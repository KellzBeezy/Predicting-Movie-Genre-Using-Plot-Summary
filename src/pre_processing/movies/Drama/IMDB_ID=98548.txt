

TITLE:	People Like Us

OVERVIEW:	It tells the story of a struggling man who, after flying home to L.A. for the funeral of his estranged record-producer father, discovers that the will stipulates that he must deliver $150,000 in cash to a 30-year-old alcoholic sister he never knew existed, and her troubled 12-year-old son.

RELEASE DATE:	29-06-2012		

GENRE: Drama
