

TITLE:	Reinventing Marvin

OVERVIEW:	Marvin, a gay actor in Paris who is struggling to write and perform a one-man theatrical show about his childhood.  Unable to fit in at school, Marvin's life was changed forever when he discovered his passion for the theatre. With no support from his family, Marvin ran away from home to chase his dreams of becoming an actor.  It is noted for an appearance by Isabelle Huppert, playing herself.

RELEASE DATE:	22-11-2017		

GENRE: Drama
