

TITLE:	Paradise Kiss

OVERVIEW:	Hyasaka Yukari only wants one thing: to enter the college that her mom wants. One day, she meets an extravagant group that loves fashion. They were searching for a model and Yukari fits their ideal model. From now Yukari will know the world of fashion and modeling, and what she really wants.

RELEASE DATE:	04-06-2011		

GENRE: Drama
