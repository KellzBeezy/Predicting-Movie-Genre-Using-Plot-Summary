

TITLE:	Goal! II: Living the Dream

OVERVIEW:	Tempted away from Newcastle United to join Real Madrid, rising star Santiago Munez finds this latest change of fortune the greatest challenge yet - personally as well as professionally. He is reunited with Gavin Harris, though they must compete to be on the team, and estranged from fiancee Roz, whose nursing career keeps her back home.

RELEASE DATE:	09-02-2007		

GENRE: Drama
