

TITLE:	Pierrepoint: The Last Hangman

OVERVIEW:	Following in his father's footsteps, Albert Pierrepoint becomes one of Britain's most prolific executioners, hiding his identity as a grocery deliveryman. But when his ambition to be the best inadvertently exposes his gruesome secret, he becomes a minor celebrity &amp; faces a public outcry against the practice of hanging. Based on true events.

RELEASE DATE:	07-12-2005		

GENRE: Drama
