

TITLE:	The Banishment

OVERVIEW:	After receiving an unexpected visit from his mysteriously injured brother Mark, Alex decides to take his family back to the country home of his childhood, but once there, Alex's wife Vera confesses that she is pregnant again and that the child is not his. Agony ensues as Alex attempts to figure out how best to react.

RELEASE DATE:	27-03-2008		

GENRE: Drama
