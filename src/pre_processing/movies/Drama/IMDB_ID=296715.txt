

TITLE:	A Pharisee

OVERVIEW:	Seung-gi (Ye Hak-yeong) has been living under a strict father, blaming himself everytime when a natural but uncontrollable desire surges from inside of him. However, because he can't spurt that desire in reality, every night Seung-gi shares passionate love with a mystery woman in his dreams.  He meets Eun-ji ("Viki") a senior from school and gets provoked by her as well as Soo-jeong (Jo Min-ah-I) who he's had a crush on forever.

RELEASE DATE:	05-09-2014		

GENRE: Drama
