

TITLE:	The Perks of Being a Wallflower

OVERVIEW:	15-year-old freshman Charlie is a wallflower, always watching life from the sidelines, until two charismatic senior students become his mentors. Free-spirited Sam and her stepbrother Patrick help Charlie discover the joys of friendship, first love, music and more, while a teacher sparks Charlie's dreams of becoming a writer.

RELEASE DATE:	20-09-2012		

GENRE: Drama
