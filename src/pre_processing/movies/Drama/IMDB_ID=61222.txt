

TITLE:	Home

OVERVIEW:	Marthe (Isabelle Huppert) and Michel (Olivier Gourmet) live with their three children in a  remote house next to an abandoned highway using it as an extension to their property. One day without warning, construction workers begin to upgrade the road and the highway becomes open to traffic. Their lives are forever changed.

RELEASE DATE:	29-10-2008		

GENRE: Drama
