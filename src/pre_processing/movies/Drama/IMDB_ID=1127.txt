

TITLE:	Princesses

OVERVIEW:	Caye is a young prostitute whose family is unaware of her profession. She meets her striking Dominican neighbour Zulema, an illegal immigrant, after she finds her in the bathroom, badly beaten up. They strike up a close friendship unbeknownst to Caye's xenophobic co-workers.

RELEASE DATE:	02-09-2005		

GENRE: Drama
