

TITLE:	Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story

OVERVIEW:	This film is a glimpse into the life, love and the unconquerable spirit of the legendary Bruce Lee. From a childhod of rigorous martial arts training, Lee realizes his dream of opening his own kung-fu school in America. Before long, he is discovered by a Hollywood producer and begins a meteroric rise to fame and an all too short reign as one the most charasmatic action heroes in cinema history.

RELEASE DATE:	07-05-1993		

GENRE: Drama
