

TITLE:	A Family Man

OVERVIEW:	Dane Jensen is a driven, Chicago-based headhunter, working at a cut-throat job placement firm. When Dane's boss announces his retirement, he pits Dane against Lynn Vogel, Dane's equally driven but polar-opposite rival at the firm, in a battle for control over the company.

RELEASE DATE:	18-05-2017		

GENRE: Drama
