

TITLE:	Through The Fire

OVERVIEW:	Franck is a firefighter in Paris. He saves people. He lives at the station with his wife, who is about to have twins. He’s happy. During a call out to a fire, be puts himself in danger, to save his men. He’s going to have to learn to live again and accept to be the one being saved, this time.

RELEASE DATE:	28-11-2018		

GENRE: Drama
