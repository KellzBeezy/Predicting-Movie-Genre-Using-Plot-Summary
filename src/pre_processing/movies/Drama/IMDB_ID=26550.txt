

TITLE:	Little Lips

OVERVIEW:	A writer returns home to his estate after service in World War I. He has developed a very bad case of depression due to a war wound concerning his genitalia. He contemplates suicide, but recovers after becoming interested in his caretaker's 12 year-old war orphan niece, who now lives in the manor.

RELEASE DATE:	08-02-1978		

GENRE: Drama
