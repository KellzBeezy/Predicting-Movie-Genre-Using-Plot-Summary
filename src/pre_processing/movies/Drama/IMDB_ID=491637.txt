

TITLE:	Mon chéri

OVERVIEW:	On a hot summer‘s day, lonely Franklin is invited to play with other kids for the first time since moving to a new village. But when the children stumble across a recently moved-in house and observe his mother, things take a drastic turn – and Franklin once again has to play a role that he is not meant for…

RELEASE DATE:	10-04-2015		

GENRE: Drama
