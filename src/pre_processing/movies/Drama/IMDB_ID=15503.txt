

TITLE:	Around the Bend

OVERVIEW:	A tale of four generations of men, all of whom have had their offspring at a young age: a great-grandfather, a grandfather, a father in his twenties and a son who is about 7 years old. When the oldest member passes away, the trio heads out on the road together in the Southwest to search out an old family secret that connects to their past.

RELEASE DATE:	08-10-2004		

GENRE: Drama
