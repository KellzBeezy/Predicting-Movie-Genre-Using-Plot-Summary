

TITLE:	The Other Woman

OVERVIEW:	Emilia, a law-school graduate, falls in love with her married boss, Jack. After Emilia marries Jack, her happiness turns unexpectedly to grief following the death of her infant daughter. Devastated, Emilia nonetheless carries on, attempting to forge a connection with her stepson William and to resist the interference of Jack's jealous ex-wife.

RELEASE DATE:	28-10-2010		

GENRE: Drama
