

TITLE:	The Stolen Caravaggio

OVERVIEW:	Valeria, young secretary of a producer, lives with an eccentric mother and secretly writes for a successful screenwriter, Alessandro. One day, she receives an unusual present from a stranger: it’s the plot of a movie about the mysterious but really-happened theft of a famous Caravaggio’s painting.

RELEASE DATE:	20-09-2018		

GENRE: Drama
