

TITLE:	Boomerang

OVERVIEW:	Antoine Rey looks for a special gift for his sister Melanie on her 40th birthday. He decides to surprise her with a trip to the French Island of Noirmoutier, where they spent many summers as children until their mother's death. On the drive back to Paris, Melanie is seriously injured in a car accident...

RELEASE DATE:	23-09-2015		

GENRE: Drama
