

TITLE:	Loro

OVERVIEW:	Internationally released Director's Cut of "Loro 1" and "Loro 2", which where released separately as two movies in Italy. The film talks about the group of businessmen and politicians – the Loro (Them) from the title – who live and act near to media tycoon and politician Silvio Berlusconi in the years between 2006 and 2009.

RELEASE DATE:	13-09-2018		

GENRE: Drama
