

TITLE:	A Fairy Tale Wedding

OVERVIEW:	After twenty years, five high school classmates meet again at Daniele's wedding, the only one who has built a career for himself. During a long weekend in Switzerland, the five friends rediscover the affection and complicity of the past, and they get a chance to straighten out their destinies. A whirlwind of misunderstandings will bring down their shaky balances, and they'll all find the courage to put themselves on the line and start their lives afresh

RELEASE DATE:	10-04-2014		

GENRE: Comedy
