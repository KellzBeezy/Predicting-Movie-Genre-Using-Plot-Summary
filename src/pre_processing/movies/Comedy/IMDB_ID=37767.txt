

TITLE:	Fun Is Beautiful

OVERVIEW:	Three stories, three characters all played by Verdone. The first is an awkward young man, who meets a Spanish tourist in Rome. The second has planned a trip towards East with a suitcase full of stockings and biro pens. The last one is a hippy whose father tries to convince him to get back home.

RELEASE DATE:	19-01-1980		

GENRE: Comedy
