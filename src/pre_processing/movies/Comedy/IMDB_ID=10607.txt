

TITLE:	Don't Be a Menace to South Central While Drinking Your Juice in the Hood

OVERVIEW:	When Ashtray (Shawn Wayans) moves to South Central L.A. to live with his father (who appears to be the same age he is) and grandmother (who likes to talk tough and smoke reefer), he falls in with his gang-banging cousin Loc Dog (Marlon Wayans), who along with the requisite pistols and Uzi carries a thermo-nuclear warhead for self-defense. Will Ashtray be able to keep living the straight life?

RELEASE DATE:	12-01-1996		

GENRE: Comedy
