

TITLE:	The Secret of My Success

OVERVIEW:	Brantley Foster, a well-educated kid from Kansas, has always dreamed of making it big in New York, but once in New York, he learns that jobs - and girls - are hard to get. When Brantley visits his uncle, Howard Prescott, who runs a multi-million-dollar company, he is given a job in the company's mail room.

RELEASE DATE:	10-04-1987		

GENRE: Comedy
