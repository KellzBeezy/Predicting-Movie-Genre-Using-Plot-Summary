

TITLE:	Moschettieri del Re

OVERVIEW:	the four heroes created by Alexandre Dumas: D'Artagnan (Pierfrancesco Favino), Athos (Rocco Papaleo), Aramis (Sergio Rubini) and Porthos (Valerio Mastandrea). Today I am an ungrammatical cattle breeder, a lustful castellan, a friar indebted and a drunken innkeeper, who will be musketeers again for patriotism.

RELEASE DATE:	27-12-2018		

GENRE: Comedy
