

TITLE:	Castello Cavalcanti

OVERVIEW:	Taking place in 1955 in Italy, “Castello Cavalcanti” follows Jason Schwartzman as race car driver Jed Cavalcanti who suffers a mishap during the Motle Miglia rally and finds himself in a small town with a few familial surprises.

RELEASE DATE:	12-11-2013		

GENRE: Comedy
