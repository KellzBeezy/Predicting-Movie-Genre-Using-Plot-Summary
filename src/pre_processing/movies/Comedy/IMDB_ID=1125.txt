

TITLE:	Dreamgirls

OVERVIEW:	Parallel love stories of three young women in Berlin: Hannah - an unhappy attorney who sexually demeans herself in order to get at least some love and romance, Leni who runs a coffee shop and has just been ditched by her long term boyfriend, and Vivienne who ends up falling in love with a puppy-eyed pasta designer with 21 dogs.

RELEASE DATE:	19-02-2015		

GENRE: Comedy
