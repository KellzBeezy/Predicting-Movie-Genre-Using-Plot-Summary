

TITLE:	Valentina... The Virgin Wife

OVERVIEW:	Valentina and Giovannino have just got married, but the Honeymoon has gone by and nothing happened between the two. Tension grows and they are about to break up the marriage, until a French tourist satisfies Valentina, while Giovannino regains his virility with his mother in law.

RELEASE DATE:	06-06-1975		

GENRE: Comedy
