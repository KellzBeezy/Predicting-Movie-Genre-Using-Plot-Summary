

TITLE:	Hallelujah!

OVERVIEW:	Following his release from a seven-year stretch in prison, Mario Diccara discovers that his affairs with the underworld aren't completely settled. His brother Patrick, a priest, suggests that he stays with elderly Father Etienne in a small village in Ardeche until the conflict blows over. But their plan takes an unexpected turn when Father Etienne dies.

RELEASE DATE:	29-04-2009		

GENRE: Comedy
