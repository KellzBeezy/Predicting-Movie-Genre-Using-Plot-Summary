

TITLE:	The Slammin' Salmon

OVERVIEW:	The brutal former heavyweight boxing champion Cleon "Slammin'" Salmon (Duncan), now owner of a Miami restaurant, institutes a competition to see which waiter can earn the most money in one night: the winner stands to gain $10,000, while the loser will endure a beating at the hands of the champ.

RELEASE DATE:	17-01-2009		

GENRE: Comedy
