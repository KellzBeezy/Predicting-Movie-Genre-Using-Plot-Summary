

TITLE:	Superfantozzi

OVERVIEW:	Superfantozzi (1986) is an Italian film from 1986. It is the fifth film in the saga of the unlucky clerk Ugo Fantozzi, played by its creator, Paolo Villaggio. In this film, Fantozzi is portrayed in a surreal historical journey, from Genesis to 1980s.

RELEASE DATE:	23-12-1986		

GENRE: Comedy
