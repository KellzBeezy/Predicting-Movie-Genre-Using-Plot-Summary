

TITLE:	My Masterpiece

OVERVIEW:	Arturo, the successful owner of an art gallery, and Renzo, a talented but decadent painter, are old friends who cannot stop bickering. One day, Arturo comes up with a solution to all their problems, but the plan they concoct turns out to be crazier and riskier than they originally expected.

RELEASE DATE:	14-08-2018		

GENRE: Comedy
