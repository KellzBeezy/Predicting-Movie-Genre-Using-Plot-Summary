

TITLE:	Beach House

OVERVIEW:	Summer Sunday at a small beach house at the coast of Rome. Many people and stories: women's basketball team, two sports-obsessed soldiers, two men with their girlfriends and the priest with a big secret, an elderly couple with their pregnant granddaughter, and engagemented couple wanting to have sex for the first time.

RELEASE DATE:	27-10-1977		

GENRE: Comedy
