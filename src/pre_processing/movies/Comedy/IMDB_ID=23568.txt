

TITLE:	American Virgin

OVERVIEW:	A Freshman College Girl on a scholarship from an abstinence group that advocates saving sex until marriage discovers that her antics on a night of debauchery, when she reluctantly got drunk for the first time in her life, were captured on camera by a sleazy video producer. Now she and her friends must travel across country to recover the incriminating footage.

RELEASE DATE:	03-10-2009		

GENRE: Comedy
