

TITLE:	National Lampoon's The Legend of Awesomest Maximus

OVERVIEW:	Spoof of sword-and-sandal flicks such as “Gladiator,” “300″ and “Troy.” Following in the long tradition of National Lampoon, this ballsy comedy takes aim at “sword and sandal” movies of every era, with s special nod of the feathered helmet to historical epics such as 300, Gladiator, Troy, and Braveheart.

RELEASE DATE:	05-08-2011		

GENRE: Comedy
