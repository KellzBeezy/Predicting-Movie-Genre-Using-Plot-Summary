

TITLE:	Black Sheep

OVERVIEW:	When dignified Albert Donnelly runs for Governor, his team moves to keep his slow-witted and klutzy younger brother, Mike, out of the eye of the media. To baby-sit Mike, the campaign assigns sarcastic Steve, who gets the experience of a lifetime when he tries to take Mike out of town during the election.

RELEASE DATE:	01-02-1996		

GENRE: Comedy
