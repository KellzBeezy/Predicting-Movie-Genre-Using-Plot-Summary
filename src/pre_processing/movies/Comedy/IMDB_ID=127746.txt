

TITLE:	La dottoressa ci sta col Colonnello

OVERVIEW:	Colonel Anacleto Punch, military doctor, falls in love with the beautiful and sensual South African Dr. Eve Russell, specializing in transplants. Thinking that his manhood is not adequate for the beautiful colleague, decides to do a transplant with the unsuspecting nurse prawn, that with women has always been successful. But the end result will not be as anticipated.

RELEASE DATE:	11-11-1980		

GENRE: Comedy
