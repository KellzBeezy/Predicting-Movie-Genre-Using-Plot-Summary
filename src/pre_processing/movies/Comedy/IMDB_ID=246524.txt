

TITLE:	Wedding Association

OVERVIEW:	Ismail's son, Tarik returns to his village after long years of being away and announces that he is engaged to a Latvian woman named Nugesha and although he doesn't have the means for it, Ismail still wants to be the one who pays for the expenses of the wedding. Several problems ensue as he tries to make sure that there is enough money for the wedding.

RELEASE DATE:	04-12-2013		

GENRE: Comedy
