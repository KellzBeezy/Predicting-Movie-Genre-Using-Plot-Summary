

TITLE:	Aziz Ansari: Buried Alive

OVERVIEW:	Standup comedian Aziz Ansari ("Parks and Recreation") headlines his third standup special, where he shares his uniquely hilarious perspective on fears of adulthood, babies, marriage, and more. Ansari's look at life on the cusp of 30 years old is smart, unfiltered, and hysterical.

RELEASE DATE:	01-11-2013		

GENRE: Comedy
