

TITLE:	The Gendarme and the Gendarmettes

OVERVIEW:	Cruchot's police office moves into a new building. They do not only get high tech equipment, but also four young female police officers to educate. All of them scramble to work with them -- and cause pure chaos while being distracted by the fine ladies. Then they get into real trouble when one after the other of their female colleagues is kidnapped.

RELEASE DATE:	03-10-1982		

GENRE: Comedy
