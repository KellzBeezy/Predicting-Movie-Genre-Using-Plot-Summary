

TITLE:	One Week

OVERVIEW:	The story involves two newlyweds, Keaton and Seely, who receive a build-it-yourself house as a wedding gift. The house can be built, supposedly, in "one week." A rejected suitor secretly re-numbers packing crates. The movie recounts Keaton's struggle to assemble the house according to this new "arrangement."

RELEASE DATE:	01-09-1920		

GENRE: Comedy
