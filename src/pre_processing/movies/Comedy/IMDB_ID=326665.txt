

TITLE:	Aziz Ansari: Live at Madison Square Garden

OVERVIEW:	Ansari headlines the iconic Madison Square Garden and delivers his most hilarious and insightful stand-up yet. Filmed in front of a sold-out audience, Ansari's latest special is an uproarious document of the comic in top form -- covering topics ranging from the struggle of American immigrants to the food industry to relationships to gender inequality.

RELEASE DATE:	06-03-2015		

GENRE: Comedy
