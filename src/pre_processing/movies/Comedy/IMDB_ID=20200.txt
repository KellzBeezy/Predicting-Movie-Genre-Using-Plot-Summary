

TITLE:	Narco

OVERVIEW:	Gustave Klopp suffers from narcolepsy, he can fall asleep everwhere at anytime whithout warning. Living a simple life with his wife Pam and his best friend Lenny Bar, Gus decides to undergo therapy sessions and finds out that he can make incredible comics from his dreams.

RELEASE DATE:	01-12-2004		

GENRE: Comedy
