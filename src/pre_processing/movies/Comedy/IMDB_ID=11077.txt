

TITLE:	Problem Child

OVERVIEW:	Ben Healy (John Ritter) and his social climbing wife Flo adopt Junior a fun-loving seven year old. But they soon discover he's a little monster as he turns a camping trip, a birthday party and even a baseball game into comic nightmares. But is he really just a little angel trying to get out? Find out in this hilarious satire on modern-day family life.

RELEASE DATE:	27-07-1990		

GENRE: Comedy
