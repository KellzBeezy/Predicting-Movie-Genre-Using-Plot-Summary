

TITLE:	She Done Him Wrong

OVERVIEW:	New York singer and nightclub owner Lady Lou has more men friends than you can imagine. One of them is a vicious criminal who’s escaped and is on the way to see “his” girl, not realising she hasn’t exactly been faithful in his absence. Help is at hand in the form of young Captain Cummings, a local temperance league leader.

RELEASE DATE:	09-02-1933		

GENRE: Comedy
