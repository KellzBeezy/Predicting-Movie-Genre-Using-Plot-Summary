

TITLE:	Furlough

OVERVIEW:	In between caring for her mother, a young woman works part time at a prison. The rookie guard gets a chance to prove her mettle when she's tasked with accompanying a hell-raising inmate on an emergency furlough to visit her dying mother. But things soon spiral out of control, sending the pair on a hilarious, surprising, and ultimately touching road trip.

RELEASE DATE:	16-03-2018		

GENRE: Comedy
