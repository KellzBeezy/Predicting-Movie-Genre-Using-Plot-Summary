

TITLE:	Muita Calma Nessa Hora

OVERVIEW:	Three young friends, Tita, Mari and Aninha decide to change their situations and travel to a beach place for a weekend. On the road, they meet Estrella, a hippie that wants a hide to try to find her unknown father. Together, they will live hilarious, absurd and moving situations. More than a change the air, they change themselves.

RELEASE DATE:	12-11-2010		

GENRE: Comedy
