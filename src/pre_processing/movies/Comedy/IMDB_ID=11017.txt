

TITLE:	Billy Madison

OVERVIEW:	Billy Madison is the 27 year-old son of Bryan Madison, a very rich man who has made his living in the hotel industry. Billy stands to inherit his father's empire but only if he can make it through all 12 grades, 2 weeks per grade, to prove that he has what it takes to run the family business.

RELEASE DATE:	10-02-1995		

GENRE: Comedy
