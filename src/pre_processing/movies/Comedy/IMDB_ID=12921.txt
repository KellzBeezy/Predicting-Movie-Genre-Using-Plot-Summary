

TITLE:	Strange Brew

OVERVIEW:	Something is rotten at the Elsinore Brewery. Bob and Doug McKenzie (as seen on SCTV) help the orphan Pam regain the brewery founded by her recently-deceased father. But to do so, they must confront the suspicious Brewmeister Smith and two teams of vicious hockey players.

RELEASE DATE:	26-08-1983		

GENRE: Comedy
