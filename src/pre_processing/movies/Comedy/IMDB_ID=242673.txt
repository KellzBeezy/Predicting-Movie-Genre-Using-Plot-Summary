

TITLE:	16 Ans ou Presque

OVERVIEW:	Arnaud Mustier, lawyer and brilliant philosopher, arouses the annoyance of his younger brother Jules, age sixteen. One day, Arnaud is a victim of a late adolescent crisis. Helped by Jules, he is going to catch up this phase of the life which he did not live.

RELEASE DATE:	18-12-2013		

GENRE: Comedy
