

TITLE:	È nata una Star?

OVERVIEW:	Plot: Marco, a shy teenager, discovers that he has what it takes to be the perfect porn star. There is only one difficulty: how does he explain this to his parents? While Marco is still thinking about how to break the news, an (anonymous) neighbor discovers a porn tape with him as a star and leaves a note in his parents’ mailbox.

RELEASE DATE:	23-03-2012		

GENRE: Comedy
