

TITLE:	Back to Mom's

OVERVIEW:	At 40, Stephanie loses her job and is forced to move back in with her mother. She is welcomed with open arms to the joys of overheated apartment, Francis Cabrel playing on loop, Scrabble games and precious motherly advice on how to behave at the table and live her life.

RELEASE DATE:	01-06-2016		

GENRE: Comedy
