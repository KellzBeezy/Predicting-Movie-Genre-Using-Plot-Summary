

TITLE:	Revenge of the Nerds II: Nerds in Paradise

OVERVIEW:	The members of the Lambda Lambda Lambda fraternity travel to Fort Lauderdale for a fraternity conference. They'll have to beat off the attacks of their rival frat, the Alphas, if they want to maintain their self-respect -- and, of course, if they want to get anywhere with the pretty girls!

RELEASE DATE:	10-07-1987		

GENRE: Comedy
