

TITLE:	Zapped!

OVERVIEW:	Peyton and Barney are fun loving high school students working on a science project with white mice. When one of the mice begins to move food toward itself with out touching it, Barney finds he has accidently discovered a formula for telekinetic powers. Now, how much trouble can a high school boy who can move things with just his mind get into?

RELEASE DATE:	23-07-1982		

GENRE: Comedy
