

TITLE:	Dead Man on Campus

OVERVIEW:	Josh Miller is a studious and responsible pre-med student entering college as a freshman. His wild, hard-partying roommate Cooper Frederickson, on the other hand, is a spoiled rich kid who never studies and spends his time getting drunk and ogling co-eds. Before long, Cooper's fun-filled lifestyle has corrupted Josh, and both are on the verge of flunking out.

RELEASE DATE:	21-08-1998		

GENRE: Comedy
