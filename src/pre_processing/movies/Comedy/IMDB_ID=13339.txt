

TITLE:	Three O'Clock High

OVERVIEW:	A high school nerd, Jerry Mitchell is assigned to write a piece for the school paper about new boy Buddy Revell, who is rumored to be a psychopathic nutcase. When Jerry accidentally touches Buddy, he says that they must fight in the parking lot at 3pm. Jerry will just about do anything to avoid the confrontation

RELEASE DATE:	09-10-1987		

GENRE: Comedy
