

TITLE:	Slap Her... She's French

OVERVIEW:	Welcome to Splendona High School, Texas, where football players, cheerleaders and beauty queens rule the hallways. And Starla Grady, the most popular girl in school, is on top of it all. That is, at least until Genevieve LePlouff, a French foreign exchange student arrives and turns her life upside down.

RELEASE DATE:	13-06-2002		

GENRE: Comedy
