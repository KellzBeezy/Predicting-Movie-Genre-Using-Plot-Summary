

TITLE:	Crash Test Aglaé

OVERVIEW:	Aglaé, a young factory worker, has only one focus in life: her job at a car crash test site. When she learns that the factory is going to be relocated abroad, she accepts, to everyone's surprise, to go to India in order to hold on to her job. Accompanied by two colleagues, she sets out on a perilous road trip to the other side of the world.

RELEASE DATE:	02-08-2017		

GENRE: Comedy
