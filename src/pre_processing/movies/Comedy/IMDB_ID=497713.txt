

TITLE:	I'm Losing It

OVERVIEW:	There's two great loves in Anya's life, her athlete  boyfriend Zhenya and food. Zhenya is not happy how Anya has been gaining weight due to her eating habits and decides to dump her. But Anya doesn't want to give up and decides to get rid of excess weight with the help of Nikolai, an overweight man obsessed with the healthy living.

RELEASE DATE:	08-03-2018		

GENRE: Comedy
