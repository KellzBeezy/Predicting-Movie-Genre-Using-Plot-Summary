

TITLE:	The Night Before the Exams

OVERVIEW:	Rome, 1989. Luca Molinari meets Claudia in a party, both of them are stressed about the upcoming final exams. Luca falls in love with the girl and now spends his time looking for her.  He is assisted by his inseparable friends:  Alice, Richard, Simon and Maximilian in his journey to find the girl he has fallen in love with.

RELEASE DATE:	17-02-2006		

GENRE: Comedy
