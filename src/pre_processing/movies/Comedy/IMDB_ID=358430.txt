

TITLE:	The New Kid

OVERVIEW:	Benoit is the new kid at a junior high school. He's bullied by a gang of arrogant boys who make things difficult for him. Determined not to be pushed around, Benoit organizes a big party and invites the whole class. But on the night of the party, only three students turn up...

RELEASE DATE:	23-12-2015		

GENRE: Comedy
