

TITLE:	Uptown Girls

OVERVIEW:	Molly Gunn, the freewheeling daughter of a deceased rock legend, is forced to get a job when her manager steals her money. As nanny for precocious Ray, the oft ignored daughter of a music executive she learns what it means to be an adult while teaching Ray how to be a child.

RELEASE DATE:	15-08-2003		

GENRE: Comedy
