

TITLE:	Picture This

OVERVIEW:	Bookish and unpopular Mandy finds her luck finally changing when Drew, the school hottie, invites her to the year's biggest party. But with being grounded and Drew's ex-girlfriend bent on thwarting her, Mandy may be destined to remain a geek. Can a little ingenuity and some help from her friends save the day?

RELEASE DATE:	13-07-2008		

GENRE: Comedy
