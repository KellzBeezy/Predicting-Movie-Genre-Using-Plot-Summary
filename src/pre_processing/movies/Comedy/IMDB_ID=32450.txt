

TITLE:	Brain Donors

OVERVIEW:	Three manic idiots—a lawyer, cab driver and a handyman—team up to run a ballet company to fulfil the will of a millionaire. Stooge-like antics result as the trio try to outwit the rich widow and her scheming big-shot lawyer, who also wants to run the ballet.

RELEASE DATE:	17-04-1992		

GENRE: Comedy
