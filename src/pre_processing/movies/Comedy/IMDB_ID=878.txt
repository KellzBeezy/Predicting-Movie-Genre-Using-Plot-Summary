

TITLE:	The Punch Bowl

OVERVIEW:	Die Feuerzangenbowle from Director Helmut Weiss is based on the novel by the same name from Heinrich Spoerl and Hans Reimann that has turned into a cult German film. The film tells the story of a writer Johannes Pfeiffer who goes undercover as a student in a high school after his friends told him that he missed out on a great life experience since he was home schooled.

RELEASE DATE:	28-01-1944		

GENRE: Comedy
