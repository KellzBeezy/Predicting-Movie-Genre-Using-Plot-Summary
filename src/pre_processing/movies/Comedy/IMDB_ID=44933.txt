

TITLE:	A Chinese in a Coma

OVERVIEW:	A tale about a talent agent with a talentless client roster. Ercole Preziosi (Verdone) represents middling girl groups, bad magicians, and even worse comics. When a grizzly traffic accident leaves his star comedian client bed-ridden, he turns to his young plucky driver to do stand-up duty

RELEASE DATE:	03-03-2000		

GENRE: Comedy
