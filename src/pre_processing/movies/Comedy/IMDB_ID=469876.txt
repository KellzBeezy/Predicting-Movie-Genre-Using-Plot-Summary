

TITLE:	Addio Fottuti Musi Verdi

OVERVIEW:	Young Cyrus Priello, an ultra-skilled Neapolitan graphic, can not find a job that satisfies and appreciates him. After sending the curriculum practically everywhere, he just have to send a copy in space. He will thus be able to work for an alien society based on competence and meritocracy.

RELEASE DATE:	09-11-2017		

GENRE: Comedy
