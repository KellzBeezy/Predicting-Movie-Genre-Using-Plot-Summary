

TITLE:	Hannibal Buress: Live From Chicago

OVERVIEW:	Hannibal is back with his hour-long stand-up special, "Hannibal Buress Live From Chicago", taped at the Vic Theatre in his hometown of Chicago, IL. Buress’ latest offering features more of the signature dry wit and cool delivery we’ve come to love.

RELEASE DATE:	24-03-2014		

GENRE: Comedy
