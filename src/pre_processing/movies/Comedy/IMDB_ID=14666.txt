

TITLE:	Cougar Club

OVERVIEW:	When Spence and Hogan graduate from college, life is bleak. They have to work for heinous divorce lawyers that torture them. Spence has a girlfriend from hell and Hogan just wants to start his life already. As luck would have it, our two young men are presented with an opportunity, they develop a club of young men devoted to the older woman, the "Cougar" if you will.

RELEASE DATE:	01-01-2007		

GENRE: Comedy
