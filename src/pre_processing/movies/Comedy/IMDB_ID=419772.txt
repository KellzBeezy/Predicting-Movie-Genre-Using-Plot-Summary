

TITLE:	Les Têtes de l'emploi

OVERVIEW:	Stéphane, Cathy and Thierry are the best employees of the Employment Agency of their city. But their results are so good that the agency will have to close because of a lack of unemployed. The three colleagues then have the crazy idea of creating unemployment to save their job.

RELEASE DATE:	16-11-2016		

GENRE: Comedy
