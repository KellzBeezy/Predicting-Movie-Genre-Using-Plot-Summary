

TITLE:	The Kitchen: World Chef Battle

OVERVIEW:	Victor Barinov at the personal request of the Russian President will go to Sochi at the world championship for professional chefs and to prove that he and his team are the best in the world! The hard struggle for the title of winner of Operations will help "dark horse" - his own son Ivan, which he suddenly found out on the eve of the championship.

RELEASE DATE:	27-04-2017		

GENRE: Comedy
