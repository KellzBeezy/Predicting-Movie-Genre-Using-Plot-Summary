

TITLE:	Everything You Always Wanted to Know About Sex *But Were Afraid to Ask

OVERVIEW:	A collection of seven vignettes, which each address a question concerning human sexuality. From aphrodisiacs to sexual perversion to the mystery of the male orgasm, characters like a court jester, a doctor, a queen and a journalist adventure through lab experiments and game shows, all seeking answers to common questions that many would never ask.

RELEASE DATE:	06-08-1972		

GENRE: Comedy
