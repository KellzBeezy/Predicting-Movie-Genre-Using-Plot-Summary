

TITLE:	100 Things

OVERVIEW:	Best friends Toni and Paul decide to relinquish all of their belongings for 100 days, whereby they receive one of their items back on each day. During this challenge the two realize, that the only thing they cannot be without is their friendship. A story about contemporary materialism and the quest for the truely important things in life.

RELEASE DATE:	06-12-2018		

GENRE: Comedy
