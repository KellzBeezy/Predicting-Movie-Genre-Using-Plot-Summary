

TITLE:	How to Get Over a Breakup

OVERVIEW:	The film tells the life of Maria Fe, a young woman who faces singleness after six years of relationship. Along with her two soul friends, played by Karina Jordán and Jely Reátegui, the girl must learn to be single again. On the way, you will run into old loves, new adventures and lots of fun.

RELEASE DATE:	31-05-2018		

GENRE: Comedy
