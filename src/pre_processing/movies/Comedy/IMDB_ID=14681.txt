

TITLE:	Eating Out

OVERVIEW:	After getting dumped by his girlfriend Tiffani (Rebekah Kochan), Caleb (Scott Lunsford) commiserates with his roommate Kyle (Jim Verraros), who notes that while he has trouble getting the men he wants he could get any woman because he's gay.

RELEASE DATE:	14-02-2004		

GENRE: Comedy
