

TITLE:	Nine and a Half Moons

OVERVIEW:	Two contrasting visions of life: on the one hand, the "rock" and adventurous of the brave Livia, cellist without maternal instinct; on the other hand, the reassuring routine of his sister Tina, shy urban vigilant with an ardent desire for maternity.

RELEASE DATE:	12-10-2017		

GENRE: Comedy
