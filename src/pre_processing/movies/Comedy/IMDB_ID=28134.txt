

TITLE:	The Impostors

OVERVIEW:	In an attempt to resurrect the slapstick comedy of Laurel and Hardy or The Marx Brothers, Stanley Tucci and Oliver Platt team-up as two out-of-work actors who accidentally stow away on a ship to hide from a drunken, belligerent lead actor who has sworn to kill them for belittling his talents.

RELEASE DATE:	18-05-1998		

GENRE: Comedy
