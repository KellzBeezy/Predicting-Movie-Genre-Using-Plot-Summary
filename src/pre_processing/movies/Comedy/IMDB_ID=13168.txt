

TITLE:	Smiley Face

OVERVIEW:	Jane (Anna Faris), a struggling but perpetually stoned actress, has a busy day ahead. She has several important tasks on her list, including buying more marijuana. Even though she already has a good start on the day's planned drug use, she eats her roommate's pot-laced cupcakes and embarks on a series of misadventures all over Los Angeles.

RELEASE DATE:	24-05-2007		

GENRE: Comedy
