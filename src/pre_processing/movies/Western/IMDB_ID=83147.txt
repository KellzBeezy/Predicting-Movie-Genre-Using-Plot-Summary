

TITLE:	Doc

OVERVIEW:	A revisionist western, "Doc" is Frank Perry's attempt to accurately portray the lives and persons of Doc Holliday, Wyatt Earp, and the now-legendary events that took place in the town of Tombstone, starring Stacy Keach, Faye Dunaway and Harris Yulin.

RELEASE DATE:	01-08-1971		

GENRE: Western
