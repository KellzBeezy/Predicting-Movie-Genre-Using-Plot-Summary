

TITLE:	Death Rides a Horse

OVERVIEW:	Bill Meceita, a boy whose family was murdered in front of him by a gang, sets out 15 years later to exact revenge. On his journey, he finds himself continually sparring and occasionally cooperating with Ryan, a gunfighter on his own quest for vengeance, who knows more than he says about Bill's tragedy.

RELEASE DATE:	31-08-1967		

GENRE: Western
