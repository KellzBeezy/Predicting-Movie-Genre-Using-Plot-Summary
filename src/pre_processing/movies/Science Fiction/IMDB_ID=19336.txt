

TITLE:	Godzilla vs. Gigan

OVERVIEW:	Godzilla's 12th film. After a accepting a job at a monster-themed park, a man learns his new employers are actually aliens who plan to use Ghidorah and Gigan to destroy earth.  Godzilla and Anguirus must battle them to defend earth.

RELEASE DATE:	12-03-1972		

GENRE: Science Fiction
