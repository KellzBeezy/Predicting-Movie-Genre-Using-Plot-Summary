

TITLE:	Parts Per Billion

OVERVIEW:	The interwoven stories of three couples which are forced to make life-altering decisions in the face of a disastrous war. Inspired and sometimes blinded by their love, Len, Mia, Andy, Esther, Anna and Erik are as flawed and beautiful as any of the billions who are facing this human-made biological disaster.

RELEASE DATE:	25-03-2014		

GENRE: Science Fiction
