

TITLE:	Doraemon: Nobita and the Robot Kingdom

OVERVIEW:	Doraemon and friends travels into another world via the time machine; where humans and robots are living together. However they soon find out that the Empress of Robot Kingdom was trying to capture robots there and turn them emotionless. As the situation goes tense, our heroes sets out to stop the Empress and her plan.

RELEASE DATE:	09-03-2002		

GENRE: Animation
