

TITLE:	Alpha and Omega: Dino Digs

OVERVIEW:	After Kate, Humphrey, and their three pups are forced to relocate their den to Wolf-burbia, they discover Amy, a friendly raptor that magically came to life after being uncovered during a big dig! The pups and their forest friends show Amy the wonders of their new world, and must work together and try to stop the diggers from unearthing the dangerous T-Rex before it's too late!

RELEASE DATE:	10-05-2016		

GENRE: Animation
