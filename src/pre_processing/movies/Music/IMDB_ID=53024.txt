

TITLE:	Elvis: Aloha from Hawaii

OVERVIEW:	A 1973 concert by Elvis Presley that was broadcast live via satellite on January 14, 1973. The concert took place at the Honolulu International Center in Honolulu and aired in over 40 countries across Asia and Europe. Viewing figures have been estimated at over 1 billion viewers world wide, and the show was the most expensive entertainment special at the time, costing $2.5 million.

RELEASE DATE:	14-01-1973		

GENRE: Music
